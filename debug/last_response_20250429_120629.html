The Nido Randwyck community is bustling with energy as students from all over the world come together in this amazing university city.

If you’re a student looking for accommodation in Maastricht, Nido Randwyck is the place to be. We’re located in the Randwyck district, just a short walk or cycle from several Maastricht University faculties, the city centre, and many other exciting things to explore while studying in Maastricht.

Nido Randwyck is home to more than 500 students and is kitted out with a series of thoughtfully-designed study and social spaces, including study rooms, gym, lounges, a private dining room, and co-working areas. You have access to a laundry room, outdoor garden, and – of course – plenty of bike storage.

We have the perfect blend of comfortable social spaces, where you can hang out with friends, and private areas, to get your head down or simply take time for yourself.

All in all, Nido Randwyck has everything you need to live your best student life in the Netherlands. Come and join the Nido community today!

Universities in Maastricht

MaastrichtUniversity

Maastricht University (UM)

Maastricht University is one of the leading universities in the Netherlands, known for its international outlook and Problem-Based Learning (PBL) approach. The Randwyck campus hosts faculties such as Health, Medicine and Life Sciences, and Psychology and Neuroscience. Nido Randwyck is about a 10-minute walk from Maastricht University's Randwyck Campus, and approximately 15 minutes by bike or a short bus ride to the city centre campuses.

MaastrichtUniversity

Maastricht School of Management (MSM)

MSM is an internationally accredited business school offering MBA, Executive MBA, and other management-related programs. It’s located close to the Randwyck area, making it convenient for Nido residents. The Maastricht School of Management (MSM) is located approximately 1.5 kilometers from Nido Randwyck, making it a convenient 15-20 minute walk. Alternatively, cycling or using public transportation can further reduce travel time.

MaastrichtUniversity

Zuyd University of Applied Science

Zuyd University offers a wide range of bachelor's and master's programs, particularly in the arts, technology, and healthcare. There are several campuses around Maastricht, with the Hotel Management School located close to Randwyck. Zuyd University is located approximately 2.5 kilometers (about 7-10 minutes by bike or a 30-minute walk) from Nido Randwyck.
